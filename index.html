About The Zine

Confetti is a literary magazine promoting the creativity of our students. Confetti will be released as a full color magazine at the end of each school year, by Mrs. Slye's Graphic Design students. Our zine showcases student art, writing and photography. All students are encouraged to submit pieces for consideration into the zine.In 2011/2012, Graphic Design 2 students will prepare the second issue of the zine, which will appear in print and be entered into competitions for Literary Magazines.

Submit Your Work

Attention all WBHS students! This year, we will be publishing a zine that will showcase all your photo, art and writing skills. All students are invited to submit - follow the instructions below. You do not have to submit something created at school for a grade, you can submit items based on your own creative ideas if you wish. However, you may submit things you've done for school, too.

Directions: please send an email to: WBHSzine@gmail.com, containing the following:

  • Title of the work
  • Media
  • A photo of your art which must be at least 1000px on the long side in order to accept.
  • Your art, photo or writing piece.
  • Click here for more information.
    • If your email is incomplete, your submission will be rejected and you will have to resend.
